Fried Egg

  • Time:15 min
  • Serves: 1 pr
  • Convenience: Easy
Ingredients Quantity Type
Egg 2 Round
Onion 1 Slice
Pepper 1 tsp Powder

Step 1: Heat the pan.
Step 2: Pour oil.
Step 3: Add onion and egg mixed.
Step 4: Add pepper and stir for 5 mins.
Step 5: Done and enjoy.
